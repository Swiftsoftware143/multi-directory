# Visitor Guide

Welcome to ZaarHub — your local community marketplace.

## Finding Businesses
1. Go to **zaarhub.com** and click your city
2. Browse categories or use the search bar
3. Each listing shows name, category, rating, contact info

## Businesses vs Suppliers
Use the tab toggle at the top of each city page:
- **Businesses** — restaurants, plumbers, dentists, shops, services
- **Suppliers** — distributors, wholesalers, farms, trade associations

They're separated so you always find what you're looking for.

## Saving Businesses (Bookmarks)
Click the ♡ heart icon on any business listing to save it to your favorites.
- The icon turns ❤️ filled when saved
- View all your saved places at **zaarhub.com/saved-places**
- Remove a bookmark anytime by clicking the heart again
- Your saved places show with name, image, category, and a quick View button
- Bookmark count appears on each listing so you can see what's popular

## Claiming Deals
1. Click a deal on a business listing
2. Click the redemption button
3. Show the code to the business to redeem
4. Deal pages have countdown timers showing how long the offer lasts

## Creating an Account (Free)
Sign up to save favorites, claim deals, leave reviews, and get city newsletters.

## Don't See Your City?
If your city isn't listed yet, you can request it:
1. Visit the directory page of the nearest listed city
2. Look for the "Request a City" section or form
3. Enter the city name and your email
4. We'll notify you when your city goes live

## Community Polls
Vote on local questions right from your directory page:
- Quick one-question polls about your community ("Best taco spot?", "Which event should we host?")
- Vote once per poll — change your vote anytime
- Live results show percentage bars after you vote
- Active polls appear in the sidebar of your city page

## Neighborhood Feed
Your personalized homepage at **zaarhub.com/feed** — everything tailored to you:
- **Greeting** — time-of-day welcome with your name
- **Saved Places** — your bookmarks at a glance, tap to revisit
- **Upcoming Events** — events you're attending, sorted by date soonest first
- **Active Polls** — vote right from the feed, see live percentages
- **Suggested Businesses** — businesses in categories you've shown interest in (from your bookmarks and survey answers)

Your feed updates as you save more businesses, RSVP to events, and vote in polls.
