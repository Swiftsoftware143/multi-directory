# Visitor Guide — Finding & Supporting Local Businesses

ZaarHub connects you with the best local businesses in your city. Discover deals, save favorites, and support your community.

## Finding Businesses

1. Go to **zaarhub.com** and click your city
2. Browse categories or use the search bar
3. Each listing shows the business name, category, rating, and contact info

## Creating a Free Account

Sign up to unlock more features:
1. Click **Sign Up** on any city page
2. Enter your email and create a password
3. You're now a ZaarHub community member

### With a free account you can:
- Save your favorite businesses
- Claim and redeem exclusive deals
- Leave reviews
- Subscribe to city newsletters
- Participate in community engagement

## Claiming Deals

1. Find a deal on a business listing page
2. Click **Claim Deal** — you'll get a unique redemption code
3. Show the code to the business to redeem
4. Deal is marked as used — simple as that

## Subscribing to Newsletters

Stay updated on what's happening in your city:
1. Scroll to the newsletter section on any city page
2. Enter your email
3. Get weekly updates on new businesses, deals, and events

## Leaving Reviews

Help other community members make informed choices:
1. Visit a business listing
2. Leave a rating and written review
3. Reviews help businesses improve and help neighbors decide

## Community Posts

Read and engage with community content:
- Local news and updates from businesses
- Event announcements
- Community discussions
- Found on your city's community page

## Supporting Local

Every time you discover a business on ZaarHub, claim a deal, or leave a review, you're supporting your local economy. Local businesses create jobs, keep money in the community, and make your city unique.

## Questions?

Contact the business directly through their listing page, or reach out to ZaarHub support for platform questions.
