🏬 Setting Up Your Loyalty Program

Everything you need to start rewarding loyal customers and growing your business with ZaarHub.

📊 1. Your Dashboard

Log into your Business Portal to access everything you need. The loyalty tab is your command center:

  • Offers — Create and manage promotions that customers redeem with credits
  • Credits — View credit earning activity and redemption history
  • Referrals — Track which customers are bringing in new participants
  • Settings — Configure your credit rate, purchase PIN, and profile
💡 Tip: Bookmark the Business Portal URL for quick access. Your session stays active for several hours.

⚙️ 2. Setting Your Credit Rate

Go to Settings in your loyalty dashboard to set how many credits customers earn per $1 spent.

  • Range: 1 to 1,000 credits per $1
  • Default: 10 credits per $1
  • Example: At 10 credits/$1, a $20 purchase earns 200 credits

Strategy tip: Higher rates attract more customers and encourage repeat visits, but cost you more when credits are redeemed against your offers. Start with the default and adjust based on your margins and goals.

💡 Tip: If you run a high-margin business (cafe, salon, services), a higher rate like 20-50 credits/$1 can be very effective. For smaller margins, 5-10 is a safe starting point.

🏷️ 3. Creating Offers

Offers are how customers redeem their credits at your business. Navigate to the Offers tab to create them.

Each offer needs:

  • Name — A clear, attractive title (e.g., "25% Off Your Purchase")
  • Discount % — The percentage off the customer's purchase total
  • Cap $ — The maximum dollar value of the discount. Example: "25% off up to $6" means the discount is 25% but never more than $6 in savings

How it works: When a customer redeems, the system calculates the percentage off their purchase total. If the discount exceeds the cap, it's capped. Credits equal to the cap value (100 credits = $1) are deducted from the customer's balance.

📝

Example: Offer "25% Off Up to $6" on a $40 purchase. 25% of $40 = $10, but capped at $6. Customer pays $34, saves $6, and 600 credits are deducted from their balance.

💡 Tip: Start with 1-2 simple offers. "10% off up to $5" and "20% off up to $10" cover most customer segments. Add seasonal offers later.

🔑 4. Your Purchase PIN

Every merchant account gets an auto-generated Purchase PIN when you sign up for the loyalty program. Format: Z100, Z101, Z102, etc.

  • Where to find it: Go to Settings in the loyalty section
  • What it's for: You enter this PIN to verify customer purchases
  • Keep it confidential: Only share with authorized staff who process verifications
  • Cannot be changed: The PIN is permanent. If compromised, contact support for a reset
⚠️ Important: Never share your PIN with customers or outside parties. Only trained staff should have access to it.

✅ 5. Verifying Purchases

When a customer wants to earn or redeem credits, use the Scanner tab in your loyalty dashboard.

1

Customer shows their QR code on their phone

2

Scan the QR code with your device's camera — the customer's ID is captured automatically

3

Enter the purchase amount and your Purchase PIN

4

Click Verify — credits are awarded automatically (or the offer is applied if redeeming)

5

Optional: If the customer wants to redeem an offer, select the offer from the dropdown before clicking Verify

💡 Tip: Make sure your camera can clearly read the QR code. Good lighting and a steady hand help. If scanning fails, you can manually type the customer's ID.

💵 6. Understanding Payouts

The ZaarHub Loyalty Program is designed to be simple and cost-effective for merchants:

  • No per-transaction fees — You don't pay anything each time a customer earns or redeems credits
  • Merchant pricing — Your subscription covers Program participation as detailed in your agreement
  • Control costs with caps — By setting offer caps (e.g., max $6 discount), you control your maximum liability per redemption
  • No minimums — No minimum transaction size to earn or redeem
💡 Tip: Review your analytics monthly to see how much discounts are costing vs. the additional revenue from repeat customers. Most merchants find the ROI strongly positive.

📋 7. Booking Requests

Visitors can request services directly from your business listing:

  • Booking Requests tab in your merchant dashboard shows incoming service requests
  • Review request details — visitor name, requested service, preferred date/time, phone, notes
  • Confirm or Decline — click to update status; visitor sees the change in their portal
  • Completed bookings automatically archive
💡 Tip: Respond to booking requests within 24 hours. Fast response times build trust and convert leads into customers.

👥 8. Referrals & Earn Zaarcash

Refer other businesses and earn Zaarcash:

  • Refer another business — earn 200 Zaarcash per referred business (2× bonus!)
  • Refer a customer — earn 50 Zaarcash when they sign up
  • Your referral link is in your merchant dashboard under Refer & Earn
  • Admin verifies the signup before Zaarcash is awarded
  • Zaarcash works like credits — usable in the ZaarHub loyalty network

🔌 9. Integration CTA Buttons

Add action buttons to your business listing from the Integrations tab:

  • Booking CTA — "Book Appointment", "Schedule a Consultation", "Book Now" — connects to CoreSwift calendar
  • Campaign CTA — "Earn Rewards", "Join Our Loyalty Program" — connects visitors to your IncentiveSwift campaign
  • SMS CTA — "Text Us", "Get Started" — triggers a FunnelSwift SMS funnel
  • Toggles and dropdowns are all controlled vocabulary — pick from pre-approved options to keep the directory consistent

⭐ 10. Best Practices

Get the most out of your ZaarHub Loyalty Program:

  • Display the ZaarHub badge — Put it on your door, counter, and website. It tells customers you participate
  • Train your staff — Make sure every cashier knows how to scan QR codes, enter amounts, and verify purchases. A 30-second training saves confusion later
  • Update offers seasonally — Change your offers every quarter to keep things fresh. Seasonal offers (summer sale, holiday special) drive engagement
  • Monitor analytics — Check which offers are most popular, what times of day customers redeem, and which customers are your biggest advocates
  • Promote the program — Mention it at checkout, on receipts, and on social media. "Earn rewards every time you shop with us!"
💡 Tip: The businesses that succeed most with loyalty programs are the ones that actively talk about it. Mention it every time a customer pays — within weeks, repeat customers will be asking for it.
