🎉 How the Loyalty Program Works for You

Earn credits when you shop. Redeem them anywhere in the ZaarHub network. It's that simple.

⚡ Quick Start

  1. Show your QR code when you pay
  2. Tell the cashier the purchase amount
  3. Credits appear in your account instantly
  4. Redeem at any participating business

🚀 1. Getting Started

Getting started with the ZaarHub Loyalty Program is quick and free. Here's what you need to know:

1

Scan a Business QR Code — When you visit a participating business, look for their ZaarHub QR code. Scanning it opens the sign-up flow directly.

2

Create Your Free Account — You need a ZaarHub account to participate. Sign up is free — do it at the business or online at the ZaarHub portal.

3

You're In! — Once signed up, you'll get your personal QR code and referral code. Start earning credits on your next purchase.

💡 Tip: No need to print anything. Your QR code lives in the visitor portal on your phone — just pull it up when you check out.

💰 2. Earning Credits

Every time you spend money at a participating business, you earn credits. The process is automatic once the business verifies your purchase.

How it works:

1

Make a purchase at any participating ZaarHub business

2

Show your personal loyalty QR code at checkout

3

The cashier scans your code and verifies the purchase amount

4

Credits are added to your account instantly

Earning rate: Each business sets their own rate. For example, if a business offers 10 credits per $1, a $5 purchase earns you 50 credits. Higher rates mean you earn faster!

💡 Tip: You can check a business's earn rate before you shop by looking at their profile in the ZaarHub directory.

📱 3. Your QR Code

Your personal QR code is your key to the ZaarHub Loyalty Program. Businesses scan this to identify you and award credits.

  • Where to find it: Log into your visitor portal and navigate to the loyalty section. Your QR code is displayed on screen.
  • Safe to share: Your QR code only contains a unique participant ID — no personal information. It's safe to show to any cashier.
  • Never share your password: Your account password is secret. Your QR code is just an identifier, not a credential.
  • Permanent: Your QR code doesn't change. Screenshot it for quick access if you like.
💡 Tip: Turn up your screen brightness so the scanner can read your QR code easily, especially in bright store lighting.

🎁 4. Redeeming Credits

Redeeming credits is where you turn your loyalty into real savings. Here's how:

1

Browse the business's Offers tab to see available discounts and rewards

2

Choose an offer — e.g., "25% off up to $6"

3

Make your purchase and tell the cashier you want to redeem an offer

4

Show your QR code. The cashier verifies the purchase and selects your chosen offer

5

Discount is applied automatically — credits are deducted, savings are yours!

Example: You have 800 credits ($8 value). You pick a "25% off up to $6" offer on a $30 purchase. Your discount is 25% of $30 = $7.50, but capped at $6, so you save $6. That's 600 credits redeemed. You keep 200 credits in your account.

💡 Tip: Keep an eye on each business's offers page. Discounts rotate seasonally, and some offers are limited-time!

👥 5. Referrals

Love the program? Share it with friends and earn bonus credits!

1

Find your referral code in the visitor portal

2

Share it with friends via text, email, or social media

3

When they sign up and complete their first verified purchase, you earn bonus credits automatically

💡 Tip: Refer friends who shop at participating businesses regularly — their first purchase triggers your bonus, and then you both earn from every visit.

📊 6. Checking Your Balance

Stay on top of your credits, vouchers, and available rewards anytime:

  • Log into the visitor portal — Your dashboard shows your current credit balance, recent transactions, and active vouchers
  • View available rewards — Browse offers from all participating businesses in one place
  • Transaction history — See every credit earned and redeemed, including which business and the date
💡 Tip: Set a reminder to check your balance weekly. You might discover new offers or see your credits growing faster than you expected!

📊 7. Community Polls

Vote on what matters in your community:

  • One-question polls appear on your directory's sidebar or feed, like "Best taco spot?" or "Which event should we host?"
  • Vote once per poll — you can change your vote anytime
  • Live results show percentage bars after you vote
  • No account needed to see poll questions and results — voting requires a free visitor login

📅 8. Upcoming Events

Discover community events in your directory:

  • Events page shows upcoming events sorted by date, with category filters
  • RSVP as Going, Maybe, or Not Going for any event
  • See who's attending — event cards show attendee counts
  • Create events — if you're a business owner, post events from your merchant dashboard
💡 Tip: RSVP early to help organizers plan. Your spot helps gauge interest!

📋 9. Service Bookings

Request services directly from businesses:

  • Request Service button on business detail pages when the business offers services
  • Fill in service type, preferred date/time, phone, and notes
  • Business confirms or declines your request — status shows on your My Bookings page
  • Cancel anytime while the request is still pending

🏠 10. Your Feed

Your personalized homepage at ZaarHub.com/feed:

  • Saved Places — your bookmarked businesses at a glance
  • Upcoming Events — events you're attending, sorted soonest first
  • Active Polls — vote right from your feed
  • Suggested Businesses — recommendations based on your bookmarks and survey answers
