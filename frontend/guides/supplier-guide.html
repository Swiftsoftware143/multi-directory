📦 Supplying Rewards on the Platform

How to list, fulfill, and manage rewards as a ZaarHub Supplier partner.

🤝 1. Your Role

As a Supplier, you're a vital part of the ZaarHub Loyalty Program ecosystem. You provide the rewards — vouchers, products, or services — that participants redeem using their earned credits.

Your rewards give participants real value and keep the program exciting. In return, you get exposure to a motivated audience of engaged shoppers and a streamlined fulfillment system.

💡 Tip: The best rewards are ones that participants genuinely want. Consider what your target audience values most and price it fairly in credits.

📋 2. Listing Rewards

Each reward listing in the ZaarHub marketplace needs clear, complete information. Here's what to include:

  • Name — A descriptive, attractive title (e.g., "$20 Gift Voucher" or "Premium Coffee Bundle")
  • Description — What the reward is, what participants receive, and any relevant details
  • Credit Cost — How many credits a participant must redeem to get this reward. Set a fair price that reflects the value you're providing
  • Terms — Expiration dates, quantity limits, geographic restrictions, and any other conditions

Fair pricing matters: If a reward has a retail value of $20, a credit cost of 2,000 ($20 equivalent) is fair. You can also offer a slight discount (e.g., 1,800 credits) to make your reward more attractive.

⚠️ Important: You must honor redemptions at the listed credit cost until you deactivate or update the listing. Do not list rewards you cannot or will not fulfill.

📦 3. Fulfilling Redemptions

When a participant redeems one of your rewards, it's your responsibility to deliver. Here's the process:

1

You receive a notification in your dashboard that a participant has redeemed your reward

2

View the redemption details — participant identification, reward selected, and delivery instructions

3

Fulfill the reward within the timeframe stated in your listing (default: 14 business days)

4

Mark the redemption as fulfilled in your dashboard once completed

You can view all pending redemptions in your dashboard at any time. Participants can contact support if fulfillment is not completed within the stated timeframe.

💡 Tip: Fulfill quickly — the faster you deliver, the more positive reviews and repeat redemptions you'll get from happy participants.

✨ 4. Quality & Standards

Maintaining high quality is essential for the Program's success. As a Supplier, you agree to:

  • Reasonable quality standards — All rewards, products, and services must meet the quality level participants reasonably expect
  • Standard remedies — Participants are entitled to remedies consistent with standard return/exchange policies in your industry
  • No misrepresentation — Your listings must accurately represent what participants receive
  • Complaint management — We may remove listings that generate excessive complaints from participants
💡 Tip: Under-promise and over-deliver. If you say "delivery in 10 business days" and deliver in 5, participants will love you.

💳 5. Fees & Settlement

Your financial relationship with the ZaarHub Loyalty Program is straightforward:

  • Supplier pricing — Governed by your separate Subscription Agreement
  • Settlement — Credit values are settled per your agreement terms
  • Platform fees — We may deduct platform fees from settlements as specified in your agreement

All fees and settlement terms are clearly outlined in your subscription documentation. Contact your account manager if you have questions about specific terms.

🔄 6. Managing Listings

You have full control over your reward listings:

  • Deactivate anytime — You can pause or remove listings from the marketplace whenever you need to
  • Update pricing — Adjust credit costs as needed (changes apply to future redemptions only)
  • Honor existing redemptions — You must fulfill any redemptions that were initiated before you deactivated a listing
  • View performance — Your dashboard shows how many times each reward has been redeemed
💡 Tip: Review your listings monthly. If a reward hasn't been redeemed in a while, try adjusting the credit cost or updating the description to make it more appealing.

❤️ 7. Bookmarks

Buyers can bookmark your supplier profile. Bookmark counts indicate serious interest — businesses comparing suppliers bookmark to revisit later.

  • Keep your product catalog accurate and descriptions detailed
  • High bookmark counts = high interest = more potential orders
