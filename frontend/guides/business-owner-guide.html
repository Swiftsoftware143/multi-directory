Everything you need to manage your business listing, ads, and subscriptions through the Business Portal.

1. Introduction

⭐ Reviews

Respond to customer reviews and manage your reputation.

⬆️ Subscriptions

Upgrade your plan for premium features and visibility.

2. Claiming Your Business

If your business is already listed in the directory, you need to claim it before you can access the Business Portal. If your business isn't listed yet, contact the directory admin or submit a new listing through the directory's submission form.

How to Claim

1

Visit your business's detail page on the public directory.

2

Click the Claim This Business button.

3

Fill in the claim form with your contact information and submit.

4

Wait for admin approval. You'll receive an email once approved.

5

Set up your Business Portal password and log in at /portal.

3. Signing In

  1. Open your browser and navigate to https://your-platform.com/portal.
  2. The login screen shows tabs for Login and Register.
  3. If you've already claimed your business, click the Login tab.
  4. Enter your email and password.
  5. Click Sign In. Your dashboard loads automatically.

💡 Tip: If you manage multiple businesses, you'll see a business selector after logging in. Select the business you want to manage from the dropdown.

First-Time Registration

If you haven't claimed your business yet but want to pre-register:

  1. Go to /portal and click the Register tab.
  2. Create your account with name, email, and password.
  3. After registration, you'll be prompted to link an existing business (search by name) or request a new listing.

5. Managing Your Plan / Subscription

Your business subscription determines the features available to you. The directory admin defines available tiers.

Viewing Your Current Plan

Your current plan is displayed in the dashboard header. It shows:

  • Plan name — e.g., Free, Basic, Premium, Enterprise
  • Status — Active, Expired, or Trial
  • Feature limits — Number of listings, photos, deals, etc.
  • Badge — Some plans include a premium badge on your listing

Upgrading Your Plan

1

Click Upgrade Plan from the dashboard or the subscriptions section.

2

Browse available tiers and compare their features and pricing.

3

Select a plan and choose monthly or annual billing.

4

Complete payment. Your plan upgrades immediately.

6. Submitting Advertisements

As a business owner, you can submit advertisements to promote your business. Ads go through an admin approval process before being displayed.

How to Submit an Ad

1

Log into the Business Portal at /portal.

2

Select your business from the dropdown (if you have multiple businesses).

3

Scroll to the 📢 Promote Your Business section.

4

Click + New Ad to open the ad submission form.

5

Fill in the following fields:

Ad NameA short, descriptive name for your ad (e.g., "Summer Special")
Slot SizeSelect from available ad zones (Hero Banner 728×90, Sidebar 300×250, Footer 468×60)
Image URLURL to your ad image. Must match the zone's dimensions.
Target URLWhere visitors go when they click your ad (optional).
6

Click Submit Ad for Review. Your ad enters the approval queue.

⚠️ Image Requirements: Your ad image must match the selected zone's exact dimensions. A 728×90 image is required for the Hero Banner zone, 300×250 for Sidebar, and 468×60 for Footer. Images that don't match will be rejected.

Available Ad Zones

Zone NameDimensionsLocationPrice
Hero Banner728 × 90 pxTop of directory pagesPer admin pricing
Sidebar300 × 250 pxSide panel on search resultsPer admin pricing
Footer468 × 60 pxBottom of directory pagesPer admin pricing

Available zones and pricing are displayed in the slot size dropdown when you create an ad.

7. Ad Status & Earnings

Viewing Submitted Ads

After submitting ads, they appear in the My Ads list section. Each ad shows:

  • Ad Name — The name you gave the ad.
  • Zone Info — Which slot size and location.
  • Status — One of three statuses:
⏳ Pending ReviewYour ad is in the approval queue. The admin needs to review it.
✅ ApprovedYour ad is approved and live on the directory. 🎉
❌ RejectedYour ad was rejected. The rejection reason is shown next to the status.

If rejected, review the reason, fix the issue, and submit a new ad.

Viewing Ad Earnings

Once your ads are approved and running, you can track earnings:

  1. Navigate to the 📢 Promote Your Business section in the portal.
  2. Locate the 💰 Ad Earnings box (it appears automatically when you have active schedules with earnings).
  3. The earnings box shows:
    • Total Earned — Cumulative earnings from all your approved ads.
    • Active Schedules — Number of ad schedules currently running.

💡 Tip: Earnings update periodically. Check back regularly to see how your ads are performing. Create multiple ads for different zones to maximize visibility.

8. Managing Reviews

Customer reviews build trust and help your business stand out. You can view all reviews and respond to them from the portal.

Viewing Reviews

Your reviews are displayed in the Reviews section of the portal.

  • Each review shows the reviewer's name, star rating, text, and date.
  • Reviews are sorted by date, newest first.
  • The dashboard header shows your total review count and average rating.

Responding to a Review

1

Go to the Reviews section in the portal.

2

Find the review you want to respond to.

3

Click Reply below the review.

4

Type your response and click Submit Reply.

5

Your reply appears publicly below the review on the business detail page.

💡 Tip: Always respond professionally to both positive and negative reviews. Thank customers for positive feedback and address concerns in negative ones. A thoughtful response shows you care about customer experience.

Review Best Practices

  • Respond quickly — Aim to respond within 24-48 hours.
  • Be professional — Keep responses courteous and helpful.
  • Address specifics — Reference what the reviewer mentioned.
  • Take it offline — For complaints, offer to discuss further via phone or email.
  • Don't argue — If a review is unfair, respond professionally without being defensive.
  • Encourage reviews — Ask satisfied customers to leave a review to build your rating.

9. Updating Business Profile

Keep your business listing accurate and up to date. A complete profile attracts more customers.

What You Can Edit

Business NameYour official business name
AddressStreet, city, state, zip code
PhonePrimary contact number
WebsiteYour business website URL
HoursDays and hours of operation
DescriptionRich text description of your business
PhotosUpload up to 10 images
CategoriesSelect or change your business category

Editing Your Profile

  1. From the portal dashboard, locate the Business Info or Profile section.
  2. Click the Edit button next to the field you want to update.
  3. Make your changes in the form that appears.
  4. Click Save to apply changes. They appear on the public listing immediately.

Uploading Photos

Photos can be uploaded from the profile editing section:

  • Click Add Photo and select an image from your device.
  • Supported formats: JPG, PNG, WebP (max 5MB each).
  • You can reorder photos by dragging them.
  • Click the delete icon to remove a photo.

💡 Tip: A complete profile with photos, updated hours, and a detailed description gets up to 3× more views than a basic listing.
