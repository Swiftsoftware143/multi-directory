👤 Visitor Back-Office Guide

Everything you need to know about your visitor account on the platform.

1. Introduction

As a visitor you can create an account to interact with directory businesses. Your account lets you:

🔖 Save Businesses

Bookmark businesses you like to quickly find them later.

⭐ Write Reviews

Share your experience with star ratings and text reviews.

🏪 Claim a Business

If you own a business listed in the directory, you can claim it as the owner.

👤 Manage Your Profile

Update your name, email, and preferences from the visitor portal.

Your visitor account is separate from the business owner portal. Use the same email to unify your experience across both.

2. Creating an Account

You can create a visitor account from the ZaarHub or any directory page. The login/register modal handles both sign-in and sign-up.

Step-by-Step

1

Click Sign In in the top navigation bar of any directory or ZaarHub page.

2

A modal appears with two tabs: Login and Register. Click the Register tab.

3

Fill in your details:

  • Name — Your display name
  • Email — Used for login and notifications
  • Password — Choose a strong password (min 8 characters)
4

Click Create Account. You'll be logged in automatically and can start using all features.

💡 Tip: Use the same email as your business owner account if you have one. This links your visitor and business profiles together.

Login

Once registered, simply click Sign In and use your email and password on the Login tab. Your session persists until you explicitly log out.

3. Saving / Bookmarking Businesses

Save businesses you're interested in to quickly find them later without searching again.

How to Save a Business

  1. Browse or search for a business in any directory.
  2. Open the business detail page by clicking on the listing.
  3. Click the 🔖 Save or ❤️ button on the business detail page.
  4. The button changes to Saved to confirm it's bookmarked.

How to Remove a Saved Business

Click the Saved button again on the business detail page to remove it from your bookmarks. You can also remove saved businesses from your Saved Places list in the visitor portal.

4. Writing Reviews

Share your experience with other visitors by leaving a review for any business in the directory.

Star Rating System

Reviews use a 5-star rating system:

⭐Poor
⭐⭐Fair
⭐⭐⭐Good
⭐⭐⭐⭐Very Good
⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐Excellent

How to Leave a Review

1

Navigate to the business detail page you want to review.

2

Scroll to the Reviews section on the page.

3

Click Write a Review button.

4

Select your star rating by clicking on the stars (1-5).

5

Write your review text describing your experience (optional but recommended).

6

Click Submit Review. Your review will appear after admin approval.

⚠️ Note: Reviews may require admin approval before they appear publicly. You can only submit one review per business. To update your review, edit it from the business detail page.

Editing or Deleting a Review

  • Edit — Go to the business detail page, find your review, and click the edit (✏️) icon.
  • Delete — Click the delete (🗑️) icon on your review to remove it entirely.

5. Viewing Saved Places

All businesses you've bookmarked are accessible from your saved places list in the visitor portal.

Accessing Saved Places

  1. Go to the Visitor Portal at /visitor or click your profile icon in the top navigation.
  2. If not logged in, sign in with your email and password.
  3. The dashboard loads, and you'll see a Saved Places section.
  4. Each saved business shows its name, category, city, and a thumbnail image.

Saved Places Features

  • View details — Click on any saved business to jump to its full profile.
  • Remove bookmark — Click the bookmark icon to remove it from your list.
  • Write a review — Click the review button to quickly leave a rating.
  • Search — Filter your saved places by name or category.

💡 Tip: Saved places sync across your sessions. Bookmark a business on your phone and find it later on your desktop.

6. Claiming a Business

If you own a business that's already listed in the directory, you can claim it to become its verified owner. This gives you access to the Business Portal where you can manage your listing, respond to reviews, and subscribe to premium features.

How to Claim

1

Navigate to the business detail page for the business you own.

2

Look for the Claim This Business button. It's usually near the top of the page or in the contact section.

3

Click the button. A claim request form appears.

4

Fill in your contact details and optionally provide proof of ownership (phone number on file, business email, or other verification).

5

Submit the claim request. It goes to the directory admin for review.

6

Once approved, you'll receive an email with instructions to set up your Business Portal password and access your business dashboard.

⚠️ Note: Claim approval is at the discretion of the directory admin. You may be asked to verify business ownership via phone, email domain, or business documents.

After Claiming

Once your claim is approved and you've set up your Business Portal credentials, you can:

  • Update your business name, address, phone, hours, and description.
  • Upload photos and manage your listing's appearance.
  • Respond to customer reviews publicly.
  • Subscribe to premium plans for enhanced visibility.
  • Submit advertisements for admin approval.

Access the Business Portal at /portal.

7. Visitor Portal

The visitor portal at /visitor is your central dashboard for managing your account, saved places, reviews, and profile settings.

Portal Sections

👤 Profile

View and edit your name, email, and avatar. Your profile is shown on reviews you leave.

🔖 Saved Places

See all businesses you've bookmarked. Click to visit their detail pages or remove bookmarks.

⭐ My Reviews

View all reviews you've written. Edit or delete them from one place.

🏪 My Claims

Track your business claim requests. See pending, approved, and rejected claims.

🔐 Security

Change your password and manage active sessions.

📧 Notifications

Configure email notifications for review replies, claim updates, and platform announcements.

Accessing the Portal

  1. Open your browser and navigate to https://your-platform.com/visitor.
  2. If not logged in, the login/register screen appears.
  3. Enter your email and password and click Sign In.
  4. Your dashboard loads with your profile card at the top.

Editing Your Profile

  1. From the dashboard, locate the Profile card or section.
  2. Click the Edit button next to your profile info.
  3. Update your name or other personal details.
  4. Click Save Changes to update your profile.

Your changes are reflected immediately, including on any reviews you've written.

Changing Your Password

  1. Navigate to the Security section in the portal.
  2. Enter your current password and your new password.
  3. Confirm the new password and click Update Password.

⚠️ Note: If you change your password, you'll need to log in again on other devices.

Logging Out

Click the Logout button in the portal header to end your session. This logs you out of all pages, not just the portal.
