Restaurant Certificates FAQs

Everything you need to know about your Dining Voucher

How do I use my Dining Voucher?

Simply present your Dining Voucher at any participating restaurant. The voucher will be applied toward your total bill. Each voucher has specific terms regarding minimum purchase and usage.

Where can I use my Dining Voucher?

Over 100,000 offers across the USA and Canada. You can browse participating restaurants at DiningAdvantage.com by entering your zip code. Offers span all dining categories and cuisines from local favorites to popular national brands.

What discounts can I expect?

Discounts of up to 50% off are available at participating restaurants. All offers are backed by Dining Advantage, powered by the Entertainment® Group.

Is there an activation fee?

Restaurant certificates have an activation fee per voucher, to be paid by the end user or prepaid by the offering business. The exact fee depends on the voucher amount and will be disclosed before activation.

How do I activate my certificate code?

Visit DiningGuru.com and follow the instructions to activate your certificate code.

Where can I view the rules of use?

The full terms of use can be viewed at DiningAdvantage.com/pages/terms-of-use.
